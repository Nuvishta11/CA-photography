About Us

CA.Photography

At CA.Photography,we specialize in wedding photography, corporate, family and senior portraits, often traveling to your destination to capture the perfect moment in the perfect place. We will be there with you every step of the way to guarantee your special moments are captured for all time. To us photography is about people being real and then letting us paint a picture of that moment to remember it forever. This is the story that matters most: real people, real stories, real moments. We are incredibly blessed to work as a team on any given days or weekend. Please take a few minutes to get to know us. We would love to visit with you by phone or in person and talk about your wedding, event, portrait session or video project. Please reach out to us through the Contact Page.

Our Services

We provide best service to our customers.

Weddings

Love recognizes no barriers. It jumps hurdles, leaps fences, penetrates walls to arrive at its destination full of hope.Of course, if it's your happily-ever-after that's being celebrated, sharing snapshots of the big day is practically mandatory. For invited wedding guests who couldn't attend, photos are the next best thing. And for those who celebrated in person, photos will forever preserve the memory.

Portrait

A photographic portrait is a picture of someone who knows she’s being photographed, and what she does with this knowledge is as much a part of the photograph as what she’s wearing or how she looks. She’s implicated in what’s happened, and she has a certain real power over the result.

Maternity Shoot

Pregnancy is a journey a very, very long journey. As exhausting as it might be, it’s also an incredibly special time in your life, and no one can blame you for wanting to capture all of the extra moments and pregnancy milestones in photos. Sharing them on your social media account with friends and family can make them feel even more exciting.

Graduation

No matter how you choose to commemorate your special day, know that it's the meaning behind the photo and the memory that really counts. Being proud of all you accomplished is always more important than the words we choose to talk about it

Fashion Shows

The power of a great outfit is impossible to overstate. At its best, fashion has the ability to transform your mood, identity, and, of course, your look. It can be fun, refreshing, and purposeful. But as with anything one does on a daily basis, it’s easy to fall into a fashion rut, feeling stale and drab no matter what you put on.

Engagements

The moment you get engaged can feel surreal and out-of-body like something out of a country love song, leaving you high on life in the moments, hours, and days to come. Once you've savored the yes with your fiancé and started to call them that for the first time!, it's time to share the joy of your news
